    Gozney Arc

    Preheat 30–40 min full flame Launch zone 340–350°C Range 340–370°C Bake 4½–5½ min Rotate every 30–45 s
    Drop flame to low before launching and keep it low. For extra colour, raise the flame for the final 15–20 seconds. Check stone temp with the infrared thermometer.

    Kenwood mixing method

    1. Mix: Speed Min, 2 min. Water, yeast and sugar in the bowl, mix briefly, then add flour. Dough hook. Stop once hydrated.
    2. Autolyse: 20 min. Cover the bowl and walk away.
    3. Develop. Add salt and olive oil. Speed 1 for 3 min, then Speed 2 for 5–6 min. Never exceed Speed 2 with the dough hook.
    4. Check. Smooth, elastic, slightly tacky, clears the bowl sides, sticks lightly at the bottom, passes the windowpane test.
    5. Rest 10–15 min, divide, ball immediately. No long warm bulk.
    6. Store. Lightly oiled tray (never floured), cover tightly, straight into the fridge.
    Target finished dough temperature: 23–25°C (acceptable 22–26°C). If it finishes above 26°C, skip any room-temperature rest and refrigerate sooner. Bench flour for stretching: 50/50 flour and fine semolina. Hand-stretch only.
    Jeet's house formula: 62% hydration · 2.5% salt · 2% oil · 1% sugar · yeast adjusted to the weather, like a proper pizzaiolo
